A Maleware Attack is a type of cyberattack where malicus software (such as viruses) are executed within a system with the intent of harm. These attacks can do anything from access personal data within your system to render your system useless.

The most common types of Maleware attcks are:
  • Viruses
  • Trojans
  • Spyware
  • Ransomeware
  • Adware (Malicious Advertising)
  • Malvertising (Pop-Ups)

Large corporations are more likely to be the target of a Ransomware attacks, where systems and data are held at ransome. However, anyone can be the victim of a maleware attack so virtually securing practices are key.
